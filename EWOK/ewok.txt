EWOK
Created by Hans Cutiongco
--------------------------------------------------------
	The Imperials have stolen the Ewoks' sacred Sunstar!  They are going to use its powers to power their new lethal weapon...THE THIRD DEATH STAR!  Chief Chirpa has assigned you to chase the nasty Imperials through foreign worlds to regain possession of the Sunstar.  Be brave little Ewok...
	     AND MAY THE FORCE BE WITH YOU!
		
How to Play
+++++++++++
*Left and Right make you go left and right.(DUH!)
*Up and Down make you climb up and down ladders and  vines.
*Shift makes you jump.
*Space makes you throw your spear.

HAVE FUN!!!!!!!
